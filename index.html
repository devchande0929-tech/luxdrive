Our Story

A passion for cars that became a promise to our customers

From a Small Garage
to Mumbai's Most Trusted
Luxury Car Dealer

LuxDrive started in 2012 with a simple idea — that buying a pre-owned luxury car should feel just as special as buying a brand new one.

Our founder, Arjun Mehta, grew up fixing cars in his father's garage in Bandra. His love for machines — especially the finely engineered ones — pushed him to start LuxDrive with just three cars, a rented space, and an unshakeable belief in honest dealing.

Over 12 years, we've grown from that tiny showroom into Mumbai's most recognised destination for certified pre-owned luxury vehicles. Every car we sell is one we'd proudly drive ourselves. That's not a slogan — it's the standard we hold every single day.

We don't just sell cars. We match people with machines that suit their life, their taste, and their budget — without pressure, without shortcuts, and without compromise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before buying your car

Are all cars certified and inspected? ▼
Yes — every single car we sell goes through a rigorous 200-point inspection covering the engine, transmission, electricals, bodywork, tyres, brakes, and interior. You get the full report before you decide to buy.
Do you offer financing / EMI options? ▼
Absolutely. We have tie-ups with leading banks and NBFCs offering competitive interest rates starting from 8.5% p.a. Use our EMI Calculator on this page to get an estimate, then our finance team will find you the best deal.
What warranty do I get with my purchase? ▼
All certified LuxDrive cars come with a minimum 1-year comprehensive warranty. Selected models come with 2-year coverage. The warranty covers major mechanical components and is honoured at our authorised service partners across Mumbai.
Can I see the car's full service history? ▼
Yes, always. We only buy cars with complete and verifiable service history. You'll receive copies of all service records, original invoice, RC book, insurance documents, and any authorised dealer service records before purchase.
Can I exchange / trade-in my current car? ▼
Yes! We accept trade-ins of most car brands. Our team will evaluate your car at fair market value and adjust it against the purchase price of your new car. Call us or visit the showroom with your car for a free valuation.
Do you deliver the car to my home? ▼
Yes, we offer home delivery across Mumbai and surrounding areas at no extra charge. Your car will be detailed, fuelled, and delivered by one of our trained staff with all documentation in hand.
How long does the buying process take? ▼
For cash purchases, delivery can happen within 24–48 hours of selecting your car. For financed purchases, it typically takes 3–5 working days for loan approval and RC transfer formalities.
